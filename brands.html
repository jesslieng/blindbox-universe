Popular Blind Box Brands From Popmart

Popmart is a market-leading character-based entertainment company and a global champion of designer toy culture.

Hirono

The artist, "Lang" got the idea from his newborn child. Through Hirono, Lang shares the message that it's okay to feel all kinds of emotions, even the hidden ones. Hirono became a voice for those feelings that often go unspoken.

Dimoo

The creator, "Ayan Deng" tells that DIMOO is more than just a blind box figure - he's a cosmic daydreamer who is shy yet warm boy that explores surreal worlds, finding magic in everyday moments.

Labubu

Labubu was created by yyartist "Kasing Lung" in 2015 as a character in his book series "The Monsters," which is inspired by Noridc folklore. The character is a kind-hearted, mischievous elf with a wide grin, known for his good intentions leading to chaotic outcomes.

A mysterious character created by artist "Xiong Miao" who deisgned to represent the concept of "non-absoluteness" and explore life's uncertainties and dualities, like life and death.
